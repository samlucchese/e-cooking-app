About Us

The App

Sam's Kitchen was only an idea back in 2020. In early 2021, I was able to bring this vision to life, with an interactive website that allows users to study and learn the many techniques of cooking, baking, grilling and more. The art of cooking can be mastered by anybody, but many people have difficulty knowing where to begin. Sam's Kitchen offers quality "courses" in the form of educational resources including articles, quizzes and videos so that you are able to start cooking easier than before. After the completion of some of these courses, you will feel like you have been cooking for lifetimes! If you are looking for some new inspiration for your next meal, feel free to check out our "Recipe" section for some tasty, awesome ideas! Throughout my childhood, my mom used to cook dinner for my siblings and I every night and so part of my inspiration for wanting to create this app comes from her. I hope to be able to pass some of the knowledge she gave to me, on to others.

Creator

My name is Sam Lucchese. I am currently a Senior at the University of Redlands in pursuit of a Computer Science degree. I am from Texas, and I love cooking good food for the people close to me, almost as much as I enjoy eating it! I was first introduced to cooking by my mom, but it wasn't until I was living on my own when I really started to discover my love for it. Other than the things I learned from her, I am a self-taught cook. When I'm not at work or in the kitchen, I enjoy spending time with friends and family, golfing, and traveling to new places.
